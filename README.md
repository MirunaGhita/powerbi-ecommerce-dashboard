## ğŸ—‚ Report Details

The report includes six main dashboard pages, each focusing on a specific business area:

**1. Sales Overview**

- Purpose: Provide high-level sales KPIs
- Key Metrics: Total Revenue, Order Count, Average Order Value, Year-over-Year Growth %.
- Visuals: Revenue trend (line chart - Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly views), Sales by category (donut chart), Top 10 products (bar chart), Order status funnel.

**2. Customer Data**

- Purpose: Understand customer behavior, segmentation, and retention.
- Key Metrics: Total Customers, Orders by Segment, Customer Repeat Rate, Total/Average Customer Lifetime Value
- Visuals: Customer segmentation (pie chart), Cohort analysis (matrix/heatmap), Cancellations by segment (bar chart), Geographic distribution (map)

**3. Product Data**

- Purpose: Analyze product sales, inventory, and profitability.
- Key Metrics: Active Products, Low Stock Items, Average Product Rating, Total Products Sold.
- Visuals: Best sellers table (with sparklines), Category performance (treemap), Product ratings histogram

**4. Marketing Data**

- Purpose: Evaluate marketing effectiveness and ROI.
- Key Metrics: Total Marketing Spend, Revenue from Campaigns, Overall ROI %, Cost per Conversion.
- Visuals: Campaign performance table, ROI by campaign (bar chart)

**5. Website Data**

- Purpose: Gain insights into digital traffic, user behavior, and conversions.
- Key Metrics: Total Sessions, Conversion Rate, Average Session Duration, Bounce Rate.
- Visuals: Traffic source performance table, Device type distribution (donut chart), SAession + Conversion rate over time (clustered column chart)

**6. Operational Performance**

- Purpose: Track logistics, fulfillment, and supplier performance.
- Key Metrics: Shipping Cost, Order Fulfillment Rate, Average Delivery Time,  Average Shipping Time, On-Time Delivery %.
- Visuals: Supplier performance table, Order Status Funnel, Fulfillment time distribution (clustered column chart)
